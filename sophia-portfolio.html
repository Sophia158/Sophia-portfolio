About

Making things
by hand & eye

I'm Sophia — a studio art major with a deep investment in the material world. My practice lives at the intersection of observation and instinct: I spend a long time looking before I begin, and even longer looking once I have.

My fine art work is rooted in traditional media — charcoal, oil, printmaking — though I'm drawn to the tension between control and accident. I'm interested in what a surface remembers: the pressure of a hand, a layer that was scraped back, a decision left visible.

Alongside my studio practice, I work on visual communication and marketing design — bringing the same careful attention to composition and intention that I bring to my fine art, just in service of ideas that need to reach people.

Based in the studio and always open to conversation about commissions, collaborations, and creative work of all kinds.
